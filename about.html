Our History

January 2020
Grand opening of the first ever outlet (headquarter) of Daily Cafe in Subang Jaya.
July 2020
Half a year had passed since the debut of Daily Cafe. We have achieved over 30,000 of sales in beverages.
December 2020
Daily Cafe has become one of the top coffee shops in the region, with over 300 visits from customers per day.
June 2021
A major milestone for Daily Cafe. A second outlet in KL Sentral has opened to provide our services to all the busy travellers.
March 2022
Daily Cafe has racked up over 500,000 sales from both outlets.
July 2022
Daily Cafe has been nominated as the best coffee shop in town.
August 2022
Another major milestone achieved. Daily Cafe has been awarded a silver prize of the best coffee shop in town, which is the first ever award received by Daily Cafe.
February 2023
The grand opening of the third outlet of Daily Cafe in Putrajaya.
May 2023
Our team of professional baristas had won 1st prize in the Regional Coffee Championship. We are truly proud of them!

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide quality and authentic coffee to our customers.
We want everyone to be able to enjoy a cup of coffee that will make them feel like it is worth the price paid.
We want to ensure that our coffee will leave a lasting impression on everyone that has tasted it, instead of just something that will be forgotten easily.
For that, the slogan of our cafe is "Authentic Coffee for All".

Our Mission

The mission of Daily Cafe is not as simple as racking up as much profit as possible.
We wish to provide real and quality coffee to everyone.
Our main goal is to refine the art of brewing coffee, instead of chasing after profit.
Of course, the wellbeing of our staff and our team is equally important in this pursuit of coffee refinement.
We care not just for our customers, but our staff as well.
They are the core part of Daily Cafe
We strive to build an environment that are fair to both employees and customers.
