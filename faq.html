Q1. What are your cafe's operating hours?
Our cafe is open from Monday to Sunday, 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
Q2. Do you offer vegetarian and vegan options?
Yes, we have a selection of vegetarian and vegan dishes on our menu.
Q3. Is Wi-Fi available at your cafe?
Yes, we offer free Wi-Fi for our customers to enjoy during their visit.
Q4. Do you take reservations?
While we do accept reservations for large groups and events, we generally operate on a first-come, first-served basis.
Q5. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payments like Apple Pay and Google Pay.
Q6. Can I bring my own food or drink to the cafe?
We kindly ask our customers not to bring outside food or beverages into the cafe.
Q7. Are pets allowed in the cafe?
Yes, we are a pet-friendly establishment and welcome well-behaved pets on our outdoor patio.
Q8. Do you offer gluten-free options?
Yes, we have gluten-free options available for those with dietary restrictions.
Q9. Are you child-friendly?
Absolutely! We have a kid's menu and a designated play area for children.
Q10. Do you offer catering services?
Yes, we provide catering services for events and special occasions. Please contact us for more details.
Q11. Can I reserve a table for a private event?
Yes, we offer private event reservations. Please get in touch with us to discuss the details.
Q12. Is there a parking facility available nearby?
Yes, there is parking available nearby. We also have bike racks for cyclists.
Q13.Do you offer gift cards?
Yes, we offer gift cards that can be purchased at our cafe.
Q14. Can I modify menu items to suit my dietary preferences?
Absolutely! Our chefs are happy to accommodate any dietary preferences or allergies.
Q15. Do you have live music or events at the cafe?
Yes, we occasionally host live music nights and special events. Check our website or social media for updates.
